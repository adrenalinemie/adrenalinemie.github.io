Step 1: The Trossachs and Argyll

By Adrien and Melanie October 4, 2020 Scotland 2020

Go west to explore a gloriously diverse area of luxuriant glens, lush forests and gardens, rolling hills and mountains, sparkling sea lochs and unspoilt beaches. Rich in biodiversity, Argyll has some of the best land, coastal and freshwater habitats and species in the country.

The Trossachs, ‘the Highlands in miniature’, border the park to the east. They form a photogenic area of sparkling lochs and steep-sided valleys, winding to a climax at Loch Katrine, one of the best in the country.
